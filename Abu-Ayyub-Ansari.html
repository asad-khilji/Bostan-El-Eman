Abu Ayyub Al Ansari

By: Dr. Omar Suleiman

Introduction

  • He was the host of the Prophet Muhammad for 7 months
  • Allah chose for Abu Ayyub to host the Prophet Muhammad
  • Abu Ayyub's name is Khalid ibn Zaid
  • Abu Ayyub purchased the entire land that is part of Masjid Al Nabi
  • Abu Ayyub is the brother of Musab ibn Umair

The Family Lineage of Abu Ayyub

  • He comes from the tribe of Banu Najar
  • The same tribe as the paternal great grandmother of Prophet Muhammad
  • The area Abu Ayyub lived is the same area Abdul Muttalib grew up in

Abu Ayyub Enters the Fold of Islam

  • Abu Ayyub is from the group that take pledge to accept the Prophet Muhammad in Madinah
  • Prophet Muhammad enters Madinah stops in Quba and builds a Masjid there
  • Then as the Prophet Muhammad enters Madinah he lets the camel sit where it will
  • Allah commands the camel to sit at the house of Abu Ayyub

The House of Abu Ayyub

  • Abu Ayyub hosts Prophet Muhammad and Sawda inside his 2 story house
  • Prophet Muhammad offers to sleep downstairs because of all the guests that would visit him
  • Abu Ayyub is nervous about the idea of being on top of Prophet Muhammad
  • Abu Ayyub and his wife slept in the corner instead of the center of the room
  • Abu Ayyub offers for the Prophet Muhammad to move upstairs
  • Prophet Muhammad tells Abu Ayyub to relax and sleep in the middle of the room
  • Abu Ayyub and his wife continued to sleep in the corner
  • One time water fell and Abu Ayyub and his wife panicked and tried to dry it
  • The next morning Abu Ayyub runs to Prophet Muhammad worred
  • Prophet Muhammad accepted Abu Ayyub's offer to move upstairs

Abu Ayyub Continues to Serve Prophet Muhammad

  • Abu Ayyub sends food to Prophet Muhammad
  • Abu Ayyub ate after Prophet Muhammad and ate from where he ate
  • One time the food came back untouched and Abu Ayyub ran downstairs
  • Abu Ayyub said is garlic haram
  • Prophet Muhammad said no but the angels do not like its smell
  • Abu Ayyub said from now on he will not serve garlic and onions
  • Abu Ayyub followed Prophet Muhammad exactly

The Miracle of Prophet Muhammad

  • Abu Ayyub prepared enough dinner for him, his wife, and two more guests
  • Prophet Muhammad said bring 30 more people
  • 30 people come in and they eat from the same meal that was prepared for 4 people
  • Then Prophet Muhammad says call 60 more people and more people
  • By the end of the day Allah fed 160 people with that little food

The Hujarat of Prophet Muhammad

  • Smaller rooms are built next to Masjid Al Nabi
  • These rooms are next to Abu Ayyub's house

When the Prophet, Abu Bakr, and Umar were Hungry

  • Prophet Muhammad did not eat much
  • He did not eat a lot like a king
  • Prophet Muhamamd came outside his house and he sees Abu Bakr and Umar
  • Prophet Muhammad said what brought you outside your home
  • They said we are hungry
  • Prophet Muhammad, Abu Bakr, and Umar go to the house of Abu Ayyub
  • Abu Ayyub and his wife welcome them into their home
  • Abu Ayyub says what brings you over to my house because it was an odd time
  • Abu Ayyub notices they are hungry and he serves them the best of food
  • Prophet Muhammad takes the food and tells Abu Ayyub to give it to Fatima Bint Muhammad
  • Prophet Muhammad sees so much blessing and starts to cry
  • Prophet says you will ask about this blessing on the day of judgment
  • Prophet said before you eat say bismillah and after you eat say Alhamdulillah

The Care Taker of the Masjid

  • Abu Ayyub was known as the care taker of the masjid
  • Abu Ayyub was the first person to throw the hypocrites outside the masjid
  • He threw them out when he heard them mock the Prophet
  • Abu Ayyub defended Ayesha during the slander
  • Allah recored his conversation in the Quran
  • Abu Ayyub heard from his wife the slander of Ayesha
  • Abu Ayyub said to his wife would you do such a thing
  • She said no so Abu Ayyub said Ayesha is better than
  • This slander is an obvious lie
  • Abu Ayyub stood outside the tent of the Prophet to protect him
  • The Prophet made dua for him to be protected as well
  • Abu Ayyub witnessed every battle with the Prophet

After the Prophet

  • Abu Ayyub continued to go to the battles
  • Abu Ayyub was in charge of Madinah when Ali went to Iraq
  • Abu Ayyub is forever associated with Madinah and the home of the Prophet
  • Abu Ayyub was on the side of Ali but he did not take part of the battles against the other Sahaba
  • Abu Ayyub joined with Ali fighting against the Khwarritte

Abu Ayyub in Iraq

  • Abdullah bin Abbas hears Abu Ayyub is coming
  • Ibn Abbas had a love for senior Sahaba
  • Ibn Abbas would sleep at the doorstep of the Sahaba
  • Ibn Abbas said I will treat you as you treated the Prophet
  • Ibn Abbas handed the keys to his house to Abu Ayyub
  • Ibn Abbas cleared all of Abu Ayyub's debt and more than that

Abu Ayyub in Egypt

  • Abu Ayyub takes care of the masjid there
  • As he does where he went
  • The Governor of Egypt was late for salah
  • Abu Ayyub told him Prophet said do not delay Maghreb until stars came out
  • Bathrooms were invented next to the homes in Egypt
  • Abu Ayyub said he was not sure what to do
  • Prophet said do not face the qibla when you relieve yourself
  • Abu Ayyub faced the other way so he does not face the qibla while relieving himself

The Grave of Abu Ayyub

  • His grave is in Istanbul, Turkey
  • Istanbul was the center of the Roman Empire
  • It was conquered in the 15th century
  • Under Muawwiah Abu Ayyub was part of the army against the Romans
  • Abu Ayyub was the closest to Madinah and now he is the fathest from Madinah
  • Abu Ayyub said as he was dying take me to the farthest gates of the city and bury me there
  • Abu Ayyub is 80 years old and he shares his last Hadith
  • Abu Ayyub narrates I heard from the Prophet say whoever dies without associating partners with Allah will enter Jannah
  • Abu Ayyub dies with sharing the glad tidings of La ila illah Allah
