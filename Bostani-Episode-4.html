Surah Al Fatihah: Episode 4

By: Ahmed Bostani

What is the Seerat?

  • The shortest and greatest way
  • A very clear path
  • It is a very vast and wide path
  • It is a exclusively designated path
  • The only path we can take
  • It connects you to your destination
  • It is a path that is upwards
  • A path that engulfs you
  • It is the one and only path

Who are on the path?

  • The Prophets
  • Those who confirmed truth in the Prophets
  • The Martyrs
  • The Righteous

The Wrong Path

  • The Path of those who had wrath upon them
    • The Jews mentioned in the Quran
    • Those who go against the scholars
    • Those who had beneficial knowledge but did not act upon it
    • Those who reject the blessings of Allah
  • The Path of the Lost
    • The Christians mentioned in the Quran
    • Those who make up things
    • Those who did not have beneficial knowledge
    • Those who did the wrong action

Why do we say Ameen?

  • We ask Allah to accept our request for guidance
  • It is not a part of the Quran
  • It is a sunnah to say Ameen after the Fatihah
