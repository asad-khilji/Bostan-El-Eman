Ibn Abbas Shadowing the Prophet

By: Dr. Omar Suleiman

Introduction

  • Abdullah Ibn Abbas has heard of the prophet for 10 years since he was a baby
  • When Ibn Abbas sees the Prophet for the first time as a 10 year old he runs to the Prophet
  • The Family of Abbas are always around the Prophet
  • Abdullah's Paternal aunt, Maymuna, is married to the Prophet

The Tahhajud of the Prophet

  • Ibn Abbas stays with the Prophet and Maymuna to see the night habit of the Prophet
  • The Prophet called Ibn Abbas the sheik of Quresh
  • Ibn Abbas slept horizontal at the feet of the Prophet and Maymuna
  • The Prophet jumped up after half the night as if he was awake all night
  • Then the Prophet wiped the sleep from his eyes then read the last 10 ayat of Ali Imran
  • Ibn Abbas prepared a bucket of wudu for the Prophet
  • The Prophet made wudu perfectly
  • The Prophet got up to pray and Ibn Abbas followed
  • The Prophet put his hand on head of Ibn Abbas and played with his ear
  • The Prophet started to pray and Ibn Abbas followed
  • The Prophet moved Ibn Abbas next to him on the right
  • Ibn Abbas moved back again and the Prophet asked him why?
  • Ibn Abbas said can anyone pray standing next you?
  • The Prophet grabbed Ibn Abbas hugged him and prayed for him
  • The Prophet prayed 13 rakah including the witr
  • The Prophet prayed with Ibn Abbas even in the house of Ayesha

The Prophet's Dua for Ibn Abbas

  • The Prophet said O Allah teach Ibn Abbas the book and the wisdom
  • This is a common dua of knowledge and wisdom
  • The Prophet hugged Ibn Abbas and asked Allah to give him understanding of the deen
  • The Prophet is very affectionate with Ibn Abbas
  • The Prophet sat next to Ibn Abbas put his hand on his head and made dua
  • May the deen be spread through him and bless him
  • When Allah wants good for someone Allah grants him wisdom
  • Ibn Abbas said One scholar is more severe on the devil than a thousand worshippers
  • The Prophet taught Ibn Abbas to have taqwa and ask only Allah

Ibn Abbas with the Prophet

  • The Prophet said do not worry about the people but only worry about Allah

  • In this time he memorizes 1,600 Hadith from the Prophet

  • But Ibn Abbas narrated it because he carried a pad and went to all Sahaba and asked them
  • One person sent to Prophet yogurt, butter, and lizard

  • Prophet drank zam zam while standing up

  • The Hadith of Ibn Abbas was very precise

  • When you ask something point it with your fingers
  • The Prophet had Abu Bakr, Umar, Khalid, and Ibn Abbas in the gathering

  • Ibn Abbas saw the Prophet making wudu

Ibn Abbas sees Jibril

  • Al Abbas was talking to the Prophet and the Prophet ignored him
  • Al Abbas got upset and left and said to his son you see what your cousin did

Hajj with the Prophet

  • Al Fadil said the Prophet kept reciting zikr during hajj then through jamarat

The Passing of the Prophet

  • Ibn Abbas was the age of 13 or 15 years when the Prophet died
  • The family of prophet washed his body at death
  • Ibn Abbas was there washing and burying the Prophet
  • Ibn Abbas does not stop his goal in Ilm
  • When the prophet died Ibn Abbas goes to his friend and said lets go to the Sahaba
  • His friend said lets just go play with the pigeons
  • Ibn Abbas left him and went to the doors of the Sahaba and fell asleep there
  • Sahaba saw Ibn Abbas sleeping there at there door
  • Sahaba said we can come to you
  • Ibn Abbas said knowledge is sought and it does not come to you

  • Zaid Bin Thabit kissed the hand of Ibn Abbas
  • Zaid said this is how we are commanded to respect our elders, teachers, and the family of the prophet
  • Ibn Abbas said I was humbled as a student and therefore respected as a student
  • Ibn Abbas friend walked by and just look at that gathering of Ibn Abbas
