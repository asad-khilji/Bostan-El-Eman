Bismillah and Al Fatihah

By: Ahmed Bostani

Bismillah

  • I begin with the name of Allah
  • My beginning is with the name of Allah
  • The first ayah revealed was Surah Al Alaq Ayah 1-5
  • Allah mentions in Surah Alaq Ayah 1 Read in the name of your Master
  • The Quran begins with the name of Allah

The Two types of Knowledge

  1. Beneficial Knowledge
    • To make yourself better
    • To improve oneself
    • To get you closer to Allah
    • To purify yourself
    • Allah requires us to use our intellect
    • Our knowledge should be accompanied with the name of Allah
  2. Non Beneficial Knowledge
    • There is a lot of information but no transformation
    • Like on social media
    • Allah repeats throughout the Quran don't you think

The Name of Allah

  • The first name of Allah is Allah
  • The word Allah is always before any of His other names
  • The name Allah is only exclusive to Allah
  • No one and nothing is like Allah
  • We worship Allah out of fear and love

Ar Rahman and Ar Raheem

  • These two names are derived from love and care
  • Ar Rahman is the second name of Allah after the name Allah
  • Allah says call on Allah then call on Ar Rahman
  • Rahman is love and care for all people
  • Raheem is love and care only for the believers
  • Ar Rahman is a title and an attribute exclusive to Allah
  • Our love and care is nothing compared to Allah's love and care
  • Allah has 100 parts of His love and care
  • One Part of Allah's love and care is for everyone in this world
  • The other 99 parts of Allah's love and care is for the believers on the day of judgement

Is Bismillah a Part of the Fatiha?

  • Imam Shafi says that Bismillah is part of the Fatihah
  • Imam Abu Hanifa, Imam Malik, and Imam Ahmad say Bismillah is not part of the Fatihah
  • Most scholars say that Bismillah is not part of the Fatihah
  • Bismillah is at the beginning of every surah except Surah At Tawbah and in one ayah in Surah Naml
  • Bismillah occurs 114 times in the Quran
  • In Hadith Qudsi Allah mentions the Fatihah and begins with Alhamdulillah and not Bismillah
  • The Prophet would say the Fatihah out loud and Bismillah silently

Surah Al Fatihah Ayah 1

  • Al Hamd means All of the Praise and Glory is for Allah
  • Rabb has several meanings
  • One of the meanings of Rabb is to raise someone
  • Another meaning of Rabb is Master
  • Alameen means anyone or anything in the universe

Surah Al Fatihah Ayah 3

  • There are two ways of reading this ayah
  • The owner of the day of judgement and the king of the day of judgment
  • The Human being can be either an Owner or a King
  • Allah is both an Owner and a King
  • Alah is the King of All Kings and the Owner of All things
  • There is no day after it
  • There is no night to follow it
  • The eternal day
  • Allah is the owner and king of this day
  • No one else owns anything on this day

The Final Day

  • There is a Hadith Qudsi
  • The angel of death has taken the souls of all people, angels, and jinn
  • Allah asks the angel of death who remains
  • The angel of death replies I remain, Jibril remains, and your noble face
  • Allah orders the angel of death to take the soul of Jibril
  • Then Allah asks the angel of death who remains
  • The angel of death replies I remain and Your Noble Face
  • Allah orders the angel of death to take his own soul
  • Nothing is left except the Noble Face of Your Master
  • Allah asks who owns dominion this day and no one answers
  • Then Allah answers Himself He is the King of All Kings and for Him is all dominion
