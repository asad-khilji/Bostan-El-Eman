Surah Al Baqarah: Episode 1

By: Ahmed Bostani

Introduction

  • This surah is the peak of the Quran
  • The Prophet said do not turn your homes into graveyards
  • The devil flees from a house in which Surah Baqarah is recited
  • Surah Al Baqarah and Surah Ali Imran will come as two huge clouds on the day of judgment
  • This surah testifies for its readers
  • This surah is a protection from black magic
  • Reading this surah is a blessing and leaving its recital is a calamity
  • Ayat ul Kursi is the greatest ayah in the Quran which is in Surah Al Baqarah
  • It is named from the incident with Musa and his people
  • When Musa told his people that Allah ordered you to slaughter a cow
  • Surah Al Baqarah is the longest surah in the Quran
  • This surah contains the longest ayah in the Quran
  • This surah talks about the believers, disbelievers, and the hypocrites

The First Ayah

  • This surah starts with three letters
  • The Laam and Meem is extended to 6 parts when reciting it
  • This is like the name of the surah
  • Only Allah knows its meaning
  • It is a sign for the people
  • It is a challenge for the people to come up with something like this Quran
  • These letters are a proof that the Prophet is being taught by someone

The Second Ayah

  • That is the book there is no doubt about it
  • It is a guide for those who are mindful of Allah
  • That is the book meaning it is the best book to come
  • No doubt because it is a miracluous and living book
  • Within this book is guidance for the pious
  • Everything about this book is true
  • There is no doubt or suspicion found in this book
  • For those who disbelieve this book is blindness

The Third Ayah

  • The ones who believe in the unseen
  • They establish their salah
  • Iqama means to be steadfast and upright
  • To establish salah means to be always be praying on time
  • And from what We have provided them they spend
  • Allah is the one who gave us everything that we spend in charity
  • Allah gave us to see what we will do with what we are given
  • Some people forget Allah is the one who provided for them

The Fourth Ayah

  • They believe in what has been revealed to you O Prophet
  • And they beleive in what was revealed much before you O Prophet
  • The Quran is higher than any other book that was sent before to the Prophets
  • We believe in all the books because it is all comingh from the same source
  • They have absolute certainty in the hereafter

The Fifth Ayah

  • Those are the ones who are truly guided by their Master
  • It is those who are the ones who are truly successful
  • This guidance is from Allah

The Sixth Ayah

  • The ones who persist in their disbelieve
  • Whether you warn them or do not warn it is all the same for them
  • They will never believe

The Seventh Ayah

  • Allah has placed a seal upon their hearts and upon their hearing
  • And on their eyes there is a covering
  • And for them is an immense punishment
  • When something is sealed nothing can go in and nothing can come out
  • Faith cannot enter their hearts
  • Disbelieve cannot escape their hearts
  • Each person is responsible for their hearts, hearing, and seeing
  • They deviated and Allah allowed them to deviate even more
  • These people chose to have their hearts sealed
  • These people knew the truth and they chose to reject it
