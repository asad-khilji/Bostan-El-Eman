Surah Al Baqarah: Ayah 18-24

By: Nouman Ali Khan

صُمٌّۢ بُكْمٌ عُمْىٌۭ فَهُمْ لَا يَرْجِعُونَ

  • They are Deaf, they cannot hear, and they cannot be heard
  • They are Mute and they cannot speak
  • They are blind, they cannot see, and they cannot be seen
  • Their ability to hear the guidance is taken away
  • Their ability to see the character of the Prophet or Muslims is taken away
  • Their ability to ask for the guidance is taken away
  • They have been abandoned
  • They are not going to return to the guidance or the fire that was lit
  • They will not be returning back to the Messenger to be guided

أَوْ كَصَيِّبٍۢ مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ فِيهِ ظُلُمَـٰتٌۭ وَرَعْدٌۭ وَبَرْقٌۭ يَجْعَلُونَ أَصَـٰبِعَهُمْ فِىٓ ءَاذَانِهِم مِّنَ ٱلصَّوَٰعِقِ حَذَرَ ٱلْمَوْتِ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ مُحِيطٌۢ بِٱلْكَـٰفِرِينَ

  • Or like dark clouds with really heavy rain that comes from the sky
  • This example is compared to the one before
  • Revelation is comforting and provides you heat and warmth
  • Revelation is also harsh and intense
  • There is also thunder and lightening
  • The warnings of the Quran are intense and scary
  • They put their fingers in their ears because of the loud sound like an explosion
  • This storm keeps on happening
  • They put their fingers in their ears out of fear of death
  • When the hypocrite hears the Quran he does not want to hear it
  • He sticks his fingers in his hears and blocks out the Quran
  • The hypocrites have lost their intellect too and they have become illogical
  • Allah is encompassing of the disbelievers
  • Allah calls the hypocrites as disbelievers
  • Because Allah knows what is in their hearts

يَكَادُ ٱلْبَرْقُ يَخْطَفُ أَبْصَـٰرَهُمْ ۖ كُلَّمَآ أَضَآءَ لَهُم مَّشَوْا۟ فِيهِ وَإِذَآ أَظْلَمَ عَلَيْهِمْ قَامُوا۟ ۚ وَلَوْ شَآءَ ٱللَّهُ لَذَهَبَ بِسَمْعِهِمْ وَأَبْصَـٰرِهِمْ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىْءٍۢ قَدِيرٌۭ

  • The lightening almost snatches their eyesight away
  • The light of revelation is so strong that they are blinded by it
  • When it lights the way for them they walk, but when it is dark they stand still
  • The hypocrites follow the revelation when it is convenient for them
  • Had Allah wanted He could have taken away their hearing and their seeing
  • Allah is over all things completely capable
  • In the previous example Allah did take their hearing and seeing away
  • But in this example they can still see and hear
  • These people still have a chance to come back to the guidance

يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلنَّاسُ ٱعْبُدُوا۟ رَبَّكُمُ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَكُمْ وَٱلَّذِينَ مِن قَبْلِكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَتَّقُونَ

  • People enslave yourselves to your Master completely in slavery and worship
  • Who created you and those much before you
  • So that you may hopefully protect yourselves

ٱلَّذِى جَعَلَ لَكُمُ ٱلْأَرْضَ فِرَٰشًۭا وَٱلسَّمَآءَ بِنَآءًۭ وَأَنزَلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءًۭ فَأَخْرَجَ بِهِۦ مِنَ ٱلثَّمَرَٰتِ رِزْقًۭا لَّكُمْ ۖ فَلَا تَجْعَلُوا۟ لِلَّهِ أَندَادًۭا وَأَنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ

  • Who made the earth for you as a comfortable floor spread out
  • Who made the earth humbled for you
  • And He made the sky as a ceiling
  • The earth was meant to be a source of protection for you
  • And He sent down water from the sky
  • Then He extracted from it all kinds of fruits
  • Then do not set up competitors with Allah
  • While you people know very well
  • Hypocrisy includes emotional or intellectual issues

وَإِن كُنتُمْ فِى رَيْبٍۢ مِّمَّا نَزَّلْنَا عَلَىٰ عَبْدِنَا فَأْتُوا۟ بِسُورَةٍۢ مِّن مِّثْلِهِۦ وَٱدْعُوا۟ شُهَدَآءَكُم مِّن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ إِن كُنتُمْ صَـٰدِقِينَ

  • And if in fact you are in any doubt about what We sent down upon our slave
  • Then bring anything remotely like a surah in the Quran
  • And call upon all your witnesses other than Allah
  • If you happen to be truthful

فَإِن لَّمْ تَفْعَلُوا۟ وَلَن تَفْعَلُوا۟ فَٱتَّقُوا۟ ٱلنَّارَ ٱلَّتِى وَقُودُهَا ٱلنَّاسُ وَٱلْحِجَارَةُ ۖ أُعِدَّتْ لِلْكَـٰفِرِينَ

  • But if you are not able to do so
  • And you will never be able to do so
  • Then protect yourselves from the fire whose fuel is men and stones
  • It has been prepared for the disbelievers and for the ungrateful
