The Prophet frequently spoke about this to Ibn Amr

The Prophet said other people feel safe from Muslims

The Prophet said it is the duty of every Prophet to warn them and give good tidings

Last part consists of trials and there are many trials

Before Prophet dies he grabs Ibn Amr and he said obey your father as long as you are alive

Ali said I am done Prophet said no just take her back to her house when it happens
