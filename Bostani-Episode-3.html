Surah Al Fatihah: Episode 3

By: Ahmed Bostani

Surah Al Fatihah Ayah 4

  • This is a transition
  • Turning our attention to Allah directly
  • We are involved in a direct conversation with Allah
  • Before we were talking about Allah
  • Now we are talking to Allah
  • Now we are in the prescence of the Divine
  • We say You alone do we worship
  • We also say You alone are we sustained by
  • The nickname of the fatihah is the mother of the book
  • This ayah is the mother of the fatihah

Why is the worship mentioned before the sustenance?

  • Some scholars say there is no difference
  • Other scholars say there is a wisdom in this
  • Worship is the goal
  • The sustenance is a means to achieve the goal
  • We are sustained by Allah in order to worship Him
  • We were not created except to worship Allah
  • We cannot worship Allah without being sustained by Allah
  • Anything that pleases Allah is worship
  • Whoever does not worship Allah, worship other than Allah
  • Our fitrah is to worship someone or something
  • We worship Allah out of love and obedience to the one who deserves to be worshipped

The 4 Categories of the People of Worship

  1. The Prophets
  2. Those who confirmed truth in the Prophets
  3. The Martyrs
  4. The Righteous

How to ask for help from Allah?

  • We receive Allah's help after we put in the effort
  • Success = Allah's help + My effort
  • A man asked the Prophet should I tie my camel or just trust Allah
  • The Prophet said tie your camel and trust Allah
  • After you have done everything you could do leave it to Allah

The 2 Ways of Asking for Help

  1. Asking Help only from Allah
  2. Asking Help from other than Allah
    1. Asking Help from someone in which only Allah can do
      • This is totally prohibited
    2. Asking help from someone while remembering Allah
      • This is permissible
    3. Asking help from someone and forgetting about Allah
      • This is totally prohibited

Surah Al Fatihah Ayah 5

  • Guide us to the straight path
  • We ask Allah to increase us in Guidance
  • We ask Allah to keep us steadfast upon guidance
  • The believers are always concerned about his or her guidance
  • We are constantly in need of Allah's guidance and advice

The 2 Types of Guidance

  1. The Guidance of being guided
  2. The Guidance of being gifted guidance

Guidance is only in Allah's Hands

  • The Prophet could show us the straight path
  • Only Allah guides whoever He wants
  • We cannot guide who we want
  • Guidance is in the hearts
  • Only Allah controls our hearts
