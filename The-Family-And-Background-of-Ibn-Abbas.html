The Family & Background of Abdullah Ibn Abbas

By: Dr. Omar Suleiman

Introduction

  • Abdullah is the son of the Uncle of the Prophet Al Abbas
  • Al Abbas is the Paternal Uncle of the Prophet
  • Abdullah's mother’s name is Um Fadil Lubaba Al Harith
  • Abdullah was born 3 years before the Hijra during the boycott of Banu Hashem in Makkah
  • No other family members were born at this time
  • When Abdullah was born they did not have much food or water
  • Abdullah did not have tahneek done to him because there were no dates
  • The Prophet held Abdullah close and made dua for him
  • Abdullah's Tahneek was the saliva of Prophet
  • The Prophet did not see Abdullah until years later when he made hijrah

The Household of the Prophet

  • The prophet had 12 uncles
  • 8 of them died before Prophet received revelation
  • 4 of them died after the Prophet received revelation
    1. Abu Lahab
      • Died in Disbelief
    2. Abu Talib
      • Died in Disbelief
    3. Al Abbas
      • He became a muslim
      • He was 3 years older than the Prophet
      • His name means lion
      • Every one loved him
      • He would bring family together
      • He did not have the status of Abu Talib because he was young
      • He was very generous with his family members
    4. Hamza
      • He was martyred as a Muslim
      • He was close to the age of the Prophet
      • His name means lion
  • When the Prophet moved to Madinah Al Abbas introduced him to the Ansar
  • Al Abbas did not think the Ansar could take care of the Prophet
  • The Ansar were very young like in there 20s
  • Al Abbas said to the Ansar are you sure you will take care of the Prophet
  • Because the Ansar would be wanted and killed by everyone
  • So the Ansar took a pledge with everything
  • Al Abbas and Abu Lahab are very close brothers
  • Abdullah bin Abbas says we were from the weak and oppressed from Makkah
  • Because they were a Muslim Household but they did not announce their Islam
  • Abdullah bin Abbas's mother accepted Islam the same day as Khadija and they both were good friends
  • Fatima bin Khattab the sister of Umar also accepted Islam with Khadija and the Mother of Abdullah bin Abbas
  • The Khala of Abdullah bin Abbas whose name was Maymuna bin Harith is the wife of the Prophet
  • The Prophet married Maymuna 6 months after hijra around the time of the treaty of hudaibia
  • Abdullah bin Abbas is the second youngest sibling and he has one younger brother Ubaidullah

The Battle of Badr

  • During the Battle of Badr the Prophet sees his uncle Abbas again
  • Unfortunately he joined the side of the disbelievers
  • Because he was forced to fight Muhammad
  • Umar bin Khattab heard Al Abbas is coming to fight at Badr
  • Umar said he will kill him
  • Prophet said O Aba Hafs
  • And it was the first time Prophet called Umar Aba Hafs so he remembers
  • He says it is really befitting that an Uncle of the Prophet be struck with a sword
  • The Prophet kept saying it to Umar
  • After Badr Al Abbas is pacing and tearing up
  • He laid down and he stayed awake
  • Prophet heard him groaning while a prisoner
  • Prophet said loosen the chains on all of the prisoners
  • All the different tribes came with ransom to free their relatives
  • No one came to ransom Al Abbas
  • The Prophet was saddened that no one came
  • Anas bin Malik said some of the Ansar came to the Prophet and said to the Prophet let us ransom Al Abbas
  • Because the Ansar were related to Al Abbas but the Prophet said no
  • Al Abbas said to the Prophet you know I accepted Islam
  • Prophet says to him Allah knows that you really are a believer
  • Allah will free you and reward you
  • What was apparent you were fighting amongst us
  • So free yourself and free your cousins Nawfal bin Harith, Ateeq bin Abu Talib, and Utbah
  • Al Abbas said to the Prophet I don’t have any money
  • Prophet says where is the money you buried
  • Al Abbas said how did you know, no body knows
  • The Prophet said ransom yourself with that money
  • Allah will reward you
  • Al Abbas said can I use the spoils of war too and the Prophet said no
  • Al Abbas ransomed himself and his cousins

After the Battle of Badr

  • Allah revealed to the Prophet
  • O Prophet say to those who are in your control of the captives
  • Allah knows the good in your hearts He will give you something better than that was taken from you
  • Allah will forgive and Allah is most forgiving and loving and caring
  • The above verse was revealed for Al Abbas
  • Al Abbas said that after Badr Allah gave me 20 servants in place of the 20 I gave to ransom with
  • Al Abbas hoped for Allah’s forgiveness
  • Abu Sufayan comes and Abu Lahab says what happened
  • Abu Sufayan said we found them small and victorious
  • They killed us and captured us
  • They were men with them that filled the sky
  • They could strike us but we could not strike them and they were referring to the angels
  • Abu Rafi said I heard and could not contain the joy in my heart
  • Abu raif jumped up and said I swear by Allah those are the angels
  • When he did that Abu Lahab beat him almost to death
  • Um Fadil saw this and took something and hit the head of Abu Lahab
  • Umm Fadil exposed his skull and the blood came down
  • Um Fadil said to Abu Lahab that do you think you can take advantage of him because his master is not here
  • The strike on the head of Abu Lahab may have caused his death a week later

The Conquest of Makkah

  • Al Abbas comes with his family and officially accepts Islam right before Fath Al Makkah in the year 630
  • Now everything is set
  • The Prophet said Abbas is from me and I am from him
  • When Abbas walked into the room the Prophet would stand up and kiss him on the forehead
  • The Prophet said this is my uncle
  • Even though Abbas is the Prophets uncle, Al Abbas addresses him as the Messenger of Allah
  • Prophet said why should I not say that when you are my uncle
  • You are what is left of my father and the uncle is like a father
  • Abbas told the Prophet Abu Sufuyan is a man of pride
  • He is Muslim but a man of pride
  • So Abbas said whenever you come to Makkah you announce places of safety
  • Make the house of Abu Sufuyan a place of safety
